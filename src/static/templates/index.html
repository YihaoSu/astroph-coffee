Welcome to Astro-Coffee@Princeton!

The Department of Astrophysical Sciences at Princeton University gathers to discuss the latest arXiv astro-ph posting every week-day at 10:30 AM US Eastern Time over coffee (and donuts/bagels on Friday!). Visitors to the department are always welcome to join in these discussions.

This astro-coffee server is meant to help organize the discussion. It automatically finds and highlights papers from local authors, and allows the community to vote for articles they want to talk about. New paper listings are fetched from the arXiv between 00:15 and 00:25 UTC every week-night & Sunday night.
